Legal Bypass — 3 Conditions (ALL must be met)
An already agreed master legal framework is in place
No new legal terms and conditions — commercial terms only
Applies to Hosted Software and Professional Services only — reviewed by internal category lead
Legal Bypass Email must include: vendor name, tool description, what it's for, term length, total cost, commercial terms negotiated
AI Enablement Process (AIAC)
Step 1: Raise a PO Request in Coupa — this initiates the procurement process
Step 2: Submit to AIAC (AI Approval Committee) for review and approval
Step 3: Procurement CANNOT proceed without AIAC approval — this is a hard gate
Step 4: Once approved — all AI T&C must be added as an amendment to the existing contract
Step 5: Flag to Legal — required regardless of whether legal bypass would otherwise apply
Coupa
All purchase requests must be raised in Coupa
Contract must be attached and approved before PO is issued
Coupa is the system of record — no purchase is authorised without a Coupa PO
SEES
Supplier & Employee Expense System — used for expense reimbursement and supplier payments outside standard PO flow
Not a substitute for Coupa — cannot be used to bypass the PO process
